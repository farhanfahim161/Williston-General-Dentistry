Your Smile is Our Priority

Williston General Dentistry has been providing patients in Williston and Levy County with excellence in dental care since 1987. We provide a stress-free, relaxing and caring environment for patients of all ages.

Using the latest technology including Digital Radiography, our experienced staff works together as a team to give you exceptional quality treatment from the moment you walk in.

Philosophy of Prevention: We believe problems can be prevented by educating our patients in proper hygiene and utilizing comprehensive, contemporary dentistry.
